Profile
I'm a second generation Greek-American, and the first in my family to graduate from college. I achieved a B.B.A. in Finance from the university of Wisconsin- Milwaukee, and am currently pursuing a Masters in Finance (M.L.A. Finance) from Harvard University. My passions are business (especially FinTech), entrepreneurship, and innovation. Beyond that, I'm also an avid software developer- certified in full-stack development from Harvard as well as Python for investment fundamentals and data analysis from Udemy. I enjoy reading, exploring new places, and trying new restaurants around town.

What I'm Reading Now
Thrive

In Thrive, Arianna Huffington wrote a passionate call to arms, looking to redefine what it means to be successful in today's world. In a commencement address she gave at Smith College in the spring of 2013, Arianna likened our drive for money and power to two legs of a three-legged stool. It may hold us up temporarily, but sooner or later we're going to topple over. We need a third leg - a Third Metric for defining success.
