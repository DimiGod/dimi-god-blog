Profile
I'm a second generation Greek-American, and the first in my family to graduate from college. I achieved a B.B.A. in Finance from the university of Wisconsin- Milwaukee, and am currently pursuing a Web Development Certificate from the Coding Bootcamp at Harvard University. My passions are business (especially FinTech), entrepreneurship, and innovation. I enjoy reading, exploring new places, and trying new restaurants around town.
